# Interview Questions (Second Interview)

## A. Firm Background

- What is the official name of the firm or office?
- How long has the firm been operating?
- Who founded the firm?
- What are the firm’s vision and mission?
- How many branches does the firm have?
- Where is the main branch located?
- Will all branches use the system?
- Will branch data be shared across branches, or kept separate?
- How many employees are there in total?
- How many employees are assigned per branch?
- Are some employees shared across branches?
- Who is the branch manager for this branch?
- Who will be the super admin?
- Who will be the admin or branch manager for the system?

## B. Services Offered

- What legal services does the firm provide?
- Does the firm offer notary services, consultation, and full case handling?
- What kinds of cases are usually handled?
- What are the most common matters the firm receives?
- Do all branches offer the same services?

## C. Client Intake and Workflow

- What happens first when a client arrives at the office?
- Who receives or interviews the client first?
- Do clients fill out forms, or does staff enter the information?
- What is the first client data recorded in the system?
- Is the client information the same for all services, or does it vary by service?
- Does every service start with client information collection?
- How is it recorded if a consultation later becomes a full case?
- What makes a case considered “accepted”?
- What makes a case considered “closed” or “terminated”?

## D. Notary Service

- What information needs to be recorded for a notary transaction?
- Are document or image uploads needed?
- Are payments recorded for notary services?
- Are notes or memos needed for notary services?
- Is notary usually a simple same-day service, or does it take longer?
- Are there different types of notary services?

## E. Consultation Service

- Is consultation recorded in the system?
- What data is needed for a consultation record?
- Are document or image uploads needed for consultation?
- Are payments recorded for consultation services?
- Are there different types of consultation?

## F. Case Management

- What information must be recorded for a case?
- What are the usual steps in a case workflow?
- Do those steps vary depending on the case?
- Is there a difference between a process and a task?
- Can one case have multiple tasks at the same time?
- Who can create a case?
- Who can edit case details?
- Who can close or terminate a case?

## G. Tasks and Deadlines

- What kinds of tasks are usually assigned?
- Who creates tasks?
- Who receives tasks?
- Do tasks belong to a case, a client, or a deadline?
- Can tasks have due dates?
- Can tasks have priorities?
- What task statuses do you want to use?
- Can notes or files be attached to tasks?
- What kinds of deadlines do you usually track?
- How many days before a deadline should reminders be sent?
- Who should receive deadline reminders?
- Do you want reminders by email, text message, or both?
- What should happen if a deadline is missed?

## H. Payments

- How are payments usually collected?
- Are payments fixed, partial, or based on service type?
- Are partial payments allowed?
- Who records payments?
- What payment statuses do you want to track?
- Do you want payment history per client or per case?

## I. Documents and Notes

- What documents are usually stored for clients and cases?
- Are document or image uploads needed?
- Where should documents be linked: to a client, case, task, or deadline?
- Who can upload documents?
- Who can delete documents?
- What kinds of notes or memos are usually added?
- Should notes be private or visible to all staff on the case?
- Should notes be linked to a case, task, or deadline?

## J. Roles and Permissions

### Super Admin

- Can add, remove, and edit users and roles
- Can assign cases to lawyers
- Can view all data across the system

### Admin / Branch Manager

- Can manage users in the branch
- Can assign cases if allowed
- Can view branch data
- Can they access other branches’ records?

### Lawyers

- Can they view only assigned cases, or all cases in their branch?
- Can they create and edit cases?
- Can they upload documents?
- Can they create tasks, notes, and deadlines?
- Can they assign tasks to paralegals or process servers?
- Can they mark tasks or cases as complete?

### Paralegals

- Can they view assigned cases only?
- Can they add notes, memos, documents, and deadlines?
- Can they mark tasks as complete?

### Process Servers

- Can they view assigned tasks only?
- Can they add notes, memos, and documents?
- Can they mark tasks as complete?

## K. Reports and Branch Access

- What reports would be useful for the firm?
- Do you want reports for pending cases, overdue deadlines, payments, or completed tasks?
- Do you want branch-specific reports?
- Should each branch only see its own records?

## L. Scope and Priorities

- What is the most important problem the system should solve first?
- Which features are essential for the first version?
- Which features can be added later?
- Is the system for internal use only?
